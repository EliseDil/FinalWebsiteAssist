Burning Curiosity Studios

BCS was created in 2020 by a particularly bored adolescent. The fires that spread from California to Oregon were dealing massive amounts of damage. Everyone was focused on fixing the damage, but nobody was focused on improvising for the future. This company was unofficially started to raise awareness in creating materials that both withstood fire or pollution if burned.

Our hours for visitors range from 8 AM to 5:30 PM on weekdays, and 11 AM to 3 PM on weekends.
